Frequently Asked Questions

What is Citymail?

Citymail is a student e-mail system based on Outlook Live Exchange. Microsoft hosts the service, while CUNY manages it, allowing each college to customize it. It offers e-mail, calendar, cloud storage, mobile access, and more.

What else do I get with Citymail?

  • Windows Office Live Workspace
  • Windows Live Messenger
  • Mobile access to IM and e-mail
  • Windows Live Expo
  • Windows Live SkyDrive
  • Access to Windows Live APIs

Is Citymail required?

Yes. Accounts are automatically created. Students are expected to check their accounts regularly for important college communications.

Why must I use my student e-mail for college business?

Your student e-mail address is uniquely assigned to you and used by over 200 administrative offices to verify your identity. It is also used to access services such as Wi-Fi, Blackboard, CUNYfirst, DegreeWorks, and more.
